# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project has adopted the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) version 2.1 as its code of conduct.

All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to uphold a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment.

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all participants regardless of background or identity.

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
